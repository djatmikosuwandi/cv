Professional Biography

Djatmiko is a PhD candidate in Educational Technology at Southwest University, Chongqing, China, and a lecturer at Universitas Negeri Surabaya (UNESA). His research is at the forefront of Generative Artificial Intelligence (GenAI) integration in higher education, specifically focusing on how AI-mediated instruction can enhance student engagement and optimize e-learning ecosystems.

With over a decade of professional experience, he has served as a strategic consultant for digital transformation at the Indonesian Ministry of Health (Kemenkes), Bank Indonesia, and the SPADA Indonesia initiative. His work bridges the gap between pedagogical theory and technological application, aiming to foster innovative and equitable learning environments globally.
